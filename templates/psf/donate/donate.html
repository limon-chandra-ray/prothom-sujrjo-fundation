Prothom Surjo Foundation is a social welfare concern voluntary organization. It is registered under Society Act, Section- XXI of 1860, and Registration no: S-11492. Our programs include- pre-schooling, sheltering of helpless girls (4-14 yrs) and rehabilitation with education and various kinds of training. We are working on educational expansion of street children, awareness rising, realization of child rights, elimination of apathy towards education. It currently is continuing sheltering of girls. The address is Dharngertek, Member Bari Culvert, Turag, Dhaka-1230. The home is running as shelter home for the underprivileged children.

We are continuing the activities of this project by collecting donation from various organization and some persons.
